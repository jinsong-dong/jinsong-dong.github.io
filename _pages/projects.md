## Commercial Systems

### PAT — Process Analysis Toolkit

A general-purpose, advanced model-checking system co-founded with two former PhD students, Sun Jun and Liu Yang, through Semantic Engineering PTE LTD. PAT comprises around one million lines of source code and supports a wide range of formal verification techniques.

- **4,000+ registered users** from over **1,000 organizations** across **150+ countries**, including Toyota, NTT, Mitsubishi, and JAXA
- **20 Year ICFEM Most Influential System Award** (2018)

### Silas — Trusted Machine Learning System

An explainable and verifiable machine-learning system co-founded with two former postdocs, Hou Zhe and Hadrien Bride, through Dependable Intelligence PTE LTD. Commercial applications span Health, Environment, and Sports Analytics.

- **50,000+ downloads**
- Project site: [www.depintel.com](https://www.depintel.com)
